What is the lean body mass calculator?
The lean body mass calculator is a tool that measures the lean body mass.
How to calculate lean body mass?
To calculate lean body mass you should use the following formula:
lean body mass (men) = 0.407 * weight [kg] + 0.267 * height [cm] - 19.2
lean body mass (women) = 0.252 * weight [kg] + 0.473 * height [cm] - 48.3
